Full Recipe In Preparing A High Protein Stew

This recipe is a budget friendly for students who want to meet their daily protein intake

Ingredients

  • 500g of Red Beans
  • 250g of Soya Chunks
  • 1kg of Chicken Breast
  • 200ml of cooking oil
  • five pieces of masala cube
  • Three pieces of onions
  • One piece of carrot
  • 200ml of tomato puree
  • 1/4g of fresh tomato
  • mix masala seasoning powder
  • 5g garlic paste
  • bell pepper

Instructions

  1. Soak the bean for 5hrs or more
  2. Boil the bean till it becomes soft
  3. boil the soya chunks with a pinch of salt for 5mins
  4. remove the salted water from the soya chunks and wash with fresh water two time and drain the water from in it
  5. wash the chicken breast and mix with two masala cube and 5g of garlic paste
  6. boil the chicked after seasoning it till it's soft
  7. place a clean pot on the fire and add the oil
  8. slice the onions, carrot,bell pepper,and tomatoes
  9. allow the oil to get hot and place the slice items in the pot and allow it fry for 10mins
  10. Add the tomato puree after the slice item has been fried
  11. After 5 - 10 mins add the beans and soya chunks and allow it to cook for 5 mins
  12. then add the chicken breast along with 500ml of water and allow it to cook for about 20-25 mins
